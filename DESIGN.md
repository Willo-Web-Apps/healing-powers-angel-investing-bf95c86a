# Design DNA — HPAI Capital

## Visual Personality: warm-luxurious
## Layout: storytelling
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Obsidian): #0D1117 → oklch(0.11 0.012 255)
- Surface (Deep Navy-Slate): #1A2332 → oklch(0.18 0.022 240)
- Surface Mid: #243044 → oklch(0.24 0.025 240)
- Accent Gold: #C9A84C → oklch(0.73 0.09 82)
- Accent Teal: #3ECFB2 → oklch(0.77 0.10 172)
- Text Primary: oklch(0.96 0.005 80) — warm off-white
- Text Muted: oklch(0.70 0.015 240)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — weights 300, 400, 600, 700 (commanding editorial serifs for headlines)
- Body: Montserrat — weights 300, 400, 500, 600 (clean, authoritative complement)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Cinematic hero vault** — multi-layer dark radial gradients with gold focal glow top-center, noise texture overlay, and a subtle conic light sweep; feels like opening a vault to something rare and precious.
2. **Three-pillar asymmetric section** — pillars use large numeral identifiers (I / II / III) in Cormorant Garamond at ~8xl with 5% opacity as decorative background letterforms, with icons and gold rule accents.
3. **Teal vs. Gold metric duality** — impact metrics rendered in healing teal, financial metrics in burnished gold, creating an instant visual language that reinforces the dual-return thesis without a single word of explanation.
4. **Section dividers as editorial flourishes** — thin gold horizontal rules (1px, 40% opacity) + generous white-space create a sense of luxury editorial rhythm, like a high-end annual report or private placement memo.
